Our Story

In 2015, we started out as a group of friends that shared the same passion for photo and videography with a mutual ambition to grow our passion into something that we could share with others in other ways than social media. Since our inception, we have helped and encouraged people from all over the country to believe in their passion and creativity. Our staff comes from different background and experiences within the fields of creativity, which makes us unique and stronger then most visual services. We offer courses and classes within a variety of fields in order to make your experience as personalised as possible.
